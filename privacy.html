Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Intimory is an iOS application designed with privacy-by-design. By default, your data is stored on your device. Optional iCloud sync (Premium) can be enabled in Settings to keep your data across Apple devices via your Apple ID. We (the developer) do not run any servers storing your personal data.

Data we process

  • Local data by default. Events, partners, notes and settings are stored locally on your iPhone. If you enable iCloud (Premium), Apple hosts encrypted copies linked to your Apple ID in your private iCloud container. We have no access to your iCloud data.
  • No tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics SDKs. Intimory does not include ads or trackers.
  • Biometrics. You can enable Face ID / Touch ID to lock the app. Biometric templates are managed by iOS and are never accessible to the app.
  • Notifications. If you enable “Smart reminders,” the app schedules local notifications on the device. No personal data is sent to external servers.

Purchases

Premium is sold via Apple’s in-app purchases (StoreKit). Payment and receipts are handled by Apple. We never receive your payment details. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings.

Retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device unless you delete it in the app or uninstall the app. If iCloud is enabled, uninstalling the app does not automatically remove your iCloud data: you can manage it in iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Minors

Intimory is intended for adults and is rated 17+ on the App Store.

Security

Data is stored in iOS sandbox. You can add a Face ID / Touch ID lock for extra protection. With iCloud, storage and transport security is provided by Apple.

International transfers

If you enable iCloud, storage and transfers are governed by Apple’s iCloud terms and infrastructure.

Contact

For questions, email us at: contact@intimory.app.

Changes

This policy may evolve. Continuing to use the app after updates means acceptance of the modified version.
